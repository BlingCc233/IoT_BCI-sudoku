Definitions

  • overhead_ratio = wire_bytes_total / payload_bytes_total (lower is better)
  • wire_entropy: Shannon entropy of wire bytes (bits/byte); random-looking ciphertext tends toward 8
  • wire_ascii_ratio: fraction of wire bytes in printable ASCII range (0..1)
  • write_size_bins_log2[i]: counts of Write() sizes within [2^i, 2^(i+1)) bytes
  • write_interarrival_ms_bins_log2[i]: counts of Write() gaps within log2(ms) bins
